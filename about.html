About

Esther Mann is an architect and ceramic artist based in [City]. Her work explores the relationship between built space and the handmade object, drawing from early memories of Hummelstown, Pennsylvania and its 19th-century brick and stone architecture.

She maintains a practice that spans residential design, interiors, and small-batch studio pottery. Each project is grounded in material honesty, careful proportion, and the quiet details of everyday life.
